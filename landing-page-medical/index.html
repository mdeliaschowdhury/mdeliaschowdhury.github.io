About Bethesda Medical

Bethesda Medical (BM) was established in Suntec City since 1995.  Recognising the limitations of solo GP practices and inherent lack of service support, Bethesda Medical was set up as a multi-doctor comprehensive Medical & Screening Centre with modern equipped facilities to provide care in a convenient location.
