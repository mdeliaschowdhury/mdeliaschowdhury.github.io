Introducing

Champion Health Plan

We are pleased to announce the Champion Health Plan, a Medicare Advantage Plan speciﬁcally designed for clients with End Stage Renal Disease (ESRD).

Champion Health Plan is an HMO POS C-SNP tailored for Medicare beneﬁciaries diagnosed with ESRD or those who have received a kidney transplant within the past ﬁve years.

Our plan serves the following counties in California:

  • Fresno

  • Imperial

  • Kern

  • Los Angeles

  • Madera

  • Orange

  • Riverside

  • San Bernardino

  • San Diego

Plan features:

  • Options for Medicare beneficiaries with Medicare only, Medicare and Medi-Cal, and Medicare with Extra Help for prescriptions

  • Point of service plan design with in-network and out-of-network benefits, tailored to meet the needs of your ESRD clients

  • $0 copayments for PCP, specialist, hospital and dialysis treatment

Great benefits beyond Medicare that include:

  • Transportation

  • Dental

  • Vision

  • Hearing Exam and Aids

  • OTC/Healthy Foods Allowance

  • Personalized Case Management
